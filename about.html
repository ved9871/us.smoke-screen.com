Technology

How the security fog system works

Simple to install, impossible to bypass. Smoke Screen fog security works by creating a complete physical zero-visibility barrier the moment an intrusion is detected.

1

Alarm Triggered

An intruder activates your existing alarm, motion detector, door contact, panic button, or CCTV trigger. No new alarm infrastructure needed.

2

Fog Machine Activates

The Smoke Screen unit receives the alarm signal and activates instantly. A specially formulated, harmless fluid is super-heated and expelled as ultra-dense security fog.

3

Zero Visibility, Property Secured

Within 30 seconds the protected space is completely filled. The intruder cannot see, cannot navigate, and cannot locate valuables. Your property is protected.

The Science Behind the Fog

Smoke Screen fog is produced by rapidly heating a water-based, glycol fluid to create a thick, harmless vapor. The fog is:

  • → Non-toxic and safe for people, pets, and property
  • → Dry, leaves no residue on electronics or goods
  • → Persistent, remains effective for up to 40 minutes unventilated
  • → Dense, reduces visibility to under 30cm in seconds
  • → Auto-dissipates naturally via standard ventilation

Integration & Installation

Smoke Screen connects to virtually any existing alarm system. No complex rewiring, the unit is triggered via:

  • → 12V or 24V trigger connection to alarm panel
  • → Remote trigger via Connectivity Box (IP-enabled)
  • → Camera-based trigger with Sentience AI platform
  • → Manual panic button override
  • → Scheduled timed activation
