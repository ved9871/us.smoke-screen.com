How It Works

Three steps. Zero intruders.

Smoke Screen integrates with your existing alarm, when it triggers, the fog does the rest. No rewiring. No complex installation.

1

Alarm Triggered

Someone trips your alarm, motion sensor, door contact, panic button. Doesn't matter which. The signal goes straight to your Smoke Screen unit.

2

Fog Deploys Instantly

The unit fires. Dense fog floods the room within seconds. By 30 seconds, visibility is zero. The fog is harmless, certified non-toxic, IEC standard, leaves zero residue.

Intruder Can't Act

Can't see, can't steal. They either flee immediately or get caught when police arrive. Your stock, your cash, your equipment, all protected.
